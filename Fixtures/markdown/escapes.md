Escaped \*asterisks\* are literal, not emphasis.

Escaped \_underscores\_ and \`backticks\` are literal.

Escaped brackets \[not a link\](nowhere) stay literal.

Escaped hash \# and pipe \| and tilde \~ characters.

A backslash before a normal char \a is kept literal.

A trailing backslash at end of paragraph\
is a hard break, not an escape.
